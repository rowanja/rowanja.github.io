Atelier MBOKA

Atelier MBOKA, meaning home-grown, something that comes naturally, is a fashion and accessories brand inspired by our natural environment and material culture to design timeless pieces for the minimally bold woman. I was responsible for creating the brand guidelines, redesigning the logo and creating packaging design.
